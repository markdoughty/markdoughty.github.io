Checklists

To help when you start University

Preparing for Uni

  1. Arrange your accomodation
  2. Make a weekly budget
  3. Look for part-time work
  4. Decide what to take with you
  5. Learn a few simple recipes
  6. Spend quality time with family and friends

What do students need to take?

  1. Bedding
  2. Essential toiletries
  3. Kitchen utensils
  4. Cleaning products
  5. Medicines
  6. Basic foods

Things to do during Welcome Week

  1. Meet other students and make friends
  2. Get to know the campus and surroundings
  3. Attend the Freshers Fair
  4. Join a society
  5. Enjoy the nightlife!
  6. Make sure the registration process is complete

How to be successful in lectures?

  1. Pay attention
  2. Share and compare notes with other students
  3. Ask questions if you are confused
  4. Arrive in good time
  5. Be prepared
  6. Skim over your notes after the lecture
